Who we are

Kianyaga High School, founded in 1961 in Kirinyaga County, is a respected national boys' school known for academic excellence. Its motto, "Enter to Excel, Exit to Prosper," reflects its mission to nurture disciplined and successful individuals. The school has notable alumni, including Deputy President Rigathi Gachagua, who has actively supported its development. In 2024, he pledged Ksh100 million for infrastructure improvements. Additionally, in 2023, alumni sponsored a retreat for teachers to strategize on enhancing the school's performance. Kianyaga High continues to thrive with strong alumni support and a commitment to quality education.
